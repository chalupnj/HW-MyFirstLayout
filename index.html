About Me

B.S. Product Design | Stanford University

I have expertise in many facets of product design, including user research, sketching, wireframing, prototyping, testing, and guiding development.

I've designed mobile and web apps, as well as responsive websites. I enjoy working closely with Product teams, and my Product Management experience gives me the unique ability to balance user needs, business goals, and engineering limitations. My academic background and skills in design thinking are strengthened by my professional experience in user empathy and UX/UI design. As a result, I have a strong foundation in user experience and product design across a variety of industries.

In my free time, I like to foster kittens and learn front-end programming languages. I combined these two hobbies and made a React app kitten matching game, which you can check out via the Kittens button below.
