About Me

I am passionate about finding innovative solutions to complex user problems using human-centered design. I graduated with a B.S. in Product Design from Stanford University and later earned a UX/UI Design Certificate at the University of Texas - Austin.

Since then, I've had 7 years experience designing B2B SaaS and B2C products. I enjoy digging into complex design problems to discover user pain points and design elegant solutions for mobile and web. I have expertise in all stages of the design process, from user research and discovery to prototyping, wireframing, testing, analysis, and guiding development.

I enjoy solving problems creatively and collaboratively, working with design partners and presenting design visions to diverse stakeholders across the company. I believe that an open line of communication between teams is vital to designing great user experiences and advancing user-centered product strategy. My history in Marketing and Product Management gives me a unique perspective and the ability to anticipate the needs of the business and of internal stakeholders.

In my free time, I like to foster animals and learn front-end programming languages. I combined these two hobbies and made a few React games, which you can check out via the Kittens and Dogs buttons below.
